About Hostel Management System

The Hostel Management System is a comprehensive web-based solution developed to efficiently manage all aspects of student hostel administration at Shri Dharmasthala Manjunatheshwar College of Engineering and Technology.

This system is built to cater to students, hostel wardens, and administrative staff, simplifying day-to-day hostel operations and improving transparency. The application ensures seamless communication and data handling while significantly reducing paperwork and manual efforts.

Key features of the system include:

  • Student Registration: New students can register by filling out a detailed form including personal, academic, and contact information.
  • Room Allocation: Hostel admins can assign rooms based on gender, sharing preference (2, 3, or 8 sharing), and availability across multiple hostels.
  • Room Swapping: Students dissatisfied with their allocated rooms can raise swap requests, which the admin can approve or reject.
  • Leave Request Management: Students can apply for leave through the portal, which is then reviewed and approved by the admin or warden.
  • Complaint Submission: Students can raise maintenance or behavioral complaints, which are tracked by the admin and can be deleted after resolution.
  • PDF Reports: The system generates downloadable PDF reports for attendance, leave applications, and more.
  • Admin Dashboard: Centralized panel for managing room allocations, monitoring complaints, reviewing leave requests, and more.
  • Secure Login System: Separate logins for students and administrators ensure role-based access and secure data management.

With a user-friendly interface and automated workflows, the Hostel Management System not only simplifies complex processes but also promotes efficient communication and record-keeping among students and administrators. It ensures that all stakeholders have access to real-time data, reducing delays and manual errors.

Our goal is to build a smart, responsive, and scalable system that enhances the overall hostel experience for everyone involved.
